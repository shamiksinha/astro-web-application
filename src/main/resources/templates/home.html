History of the Temple and Introduction of Dynasty

When Shri Panchanan Tarkalankar was arrived from Mithila to Tribeni Snagam in the estimated year of 1785, since then the journey of this dynasty began. The idol of the temple was shifted from Mithila to Tribeni Snagam. From then onwards the lineage of this dynasty was blessed by the almighty and the temple was fully enlightened. Shri Panchanan Tarkalankar’s son Shri Ramkishore Tarkalankar and his son Ramkrishan Nayratna shifted from Tribeni Sangam to Chandan Nagar. The idol of the temple was also shifted and established the temple in Chandan Nagar. The present temple and the house was established by Shri Panchanan Bhattacharya and his son Ram Ram Bhattacharya.
In the year 1915 Shri Baulchand Bharracharya and Pranchor Bhattacharya established the “Soubhagya Dishari Protisthan”. Shri Bojoy Chandra Bhattacharya’s son Shri Anadi Nath Bhattacharya got the dream from the divine goddess to spread the “Soubhagya Dishari Protisthan” in and around the country. Shri Somnath Bhattacharya’s relentless effort and tireless labour has helped him to spread the blessings of the divine goddess Mahalakshmi in the country and abroad. To fulfil his father’s desire Shri Somnath Bhattacharya started maintaining the lineage of his ancestors he gave priority to the services for human being those are suffering from severe financial problem. He established several branches of this temple and offering this holy service for the financial stability and prosperity of business of the mankind.
